How to Play

Each level shows pizzas in different positions 20 times. Catch them by:

  • Clicking the pizza when it appears.
  • Or using the arrow keys: Up for top, Down for bottom, Left for left, Right for right.
  • The center pizza can be caught by clicking it or by pressing 0 (zero) on your keyboard. Arrow keys do not catch center.

Speed increases each level. After 20 appearances the level ends and you see results.
